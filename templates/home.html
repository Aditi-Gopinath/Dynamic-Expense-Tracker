Welcome to Our Expense Tracker Web App

Our Expense Tracker Web App is designed to simplify the process of managing your finances effectively. With its intuitive interface and powerful features, tracking your expenses has never been easier.

Key Features

  • Easy Expense Entry : Quickly add your expenses with just a few clicks. Enter details such as date, amount, category, and description effortlessly.
  • Customizable Categories : Tailor your expense categories to suit your specific needs. Whether it's groceries, utilities, or entertainment, you can organize your expenses exactly how you want.
  • Visual Insights : Gain valuable insights into your spending habits with our interactive charts and graphs. Visual representations help you understand where your money is going at a glance.
  • Secure and Private : Rest assured that your financial data is safe and secure with our robust security measures. We prioritize the privacy of your information.

    • How It Works

  • Stay Informed : Receive notifications when you're approaching your budget limits or when important payments are due. Never miss a bill again.
